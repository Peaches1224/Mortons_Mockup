Mortons Steak House

the best STEAK anywhere!

As you step into the restaurant, you're greeted by a warm and inviting ambiance, with rich wood paneling, plush leather booths, and dim lighting creating an atmosphere of timeless luxury. The restaurant's interior design strikes a balance between modern sophistication and traditional steakhouse charm, setting the stage for an unforgettable dining experience. The centerpiece of Morton's menu is, of course, its selection of prime-aged steaks, expertly prepared and cooked to perfection. From the melt-in-your-mouth filet mignon to the richly marbled ribeye, each cut is hand-selected and meticulously grilled to your desired level of doneness. Accompaniments such as buttery mashed potatoes, creamy spinach, and decadent lobster macaroni and cheese complement the steaks perfectly, creating a truly indulgent meal. In addition to its steak offerings, Morton's also features an extensive selection of seafood, including fresh oysters, succulent lobster tails, and delicate shrimp cocktail, providing plenty of options for diners with varied tastes. To enhance the dining experience, Morton's offers an impressive wine list featuring a curated selection of reds, whites, and sparkling wines from around the world. Knowledgeable sommeliers are on hand to help you find the perfect pairing for your meal, ensuring that every sip complements the flavors of your steak or seafood dish. Service at Morton's is attentive and polished, with a team of professional servers dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience from start to finish. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, entertaining clients, or simply indulging in a memorable meal, Morton's The Steakhouse in San Francisco offers a sophisticated setting and unparalleled culinary excellence that is sure to impress.
